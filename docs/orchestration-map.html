    Evidence triggers

    1. 01Likely search space exceeds eight files.
    2. 02More than ten files changed.
    3. 03Executor reporting is missing or inconsistent.
    4. 04API, schema, SQL, or broad call sites need inventory.
    5. 05A broad review packet needs compression.

    Risk review triggers

    1. 01Security, auth, persistence, migration, or data-loss exposure.
    2. 02API/schema, analytics, concurrency, or infrastructure changes.
    3. 03User-visible, cross-module, multi-file, or weakly verified behavior.
    4. 04Dependencies, lockfiles, broad refactors, or ambiguous requirements.
    5. 05Reviewer disagreement, escalation, or unresolved confidence gaps.
    Single control plane

    The orchestrator is read-only and owns decomposition, parallelism, recovery, review routing, and the final answer.

    Canonical state comes from verification

    Executor reports are checked against exact commands, final git status, final diff, tests, failures, and untracked files.

    One bounded correction cycle

    Failed reporting or review findings produce a smaller executor packet, mandatory re-verification, and one affected re-review.
